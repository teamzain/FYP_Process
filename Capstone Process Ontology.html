Abstract

This ontology describes key aspects of a capstone project, including general information, team roles, supervision, documentation, project phases, technical details, testing, outcomes, academic context, presentations, tools, and legal/ethical considerations. It aims to provide a structured representation for querying and reasoning about capstone projects.
